About Us

Hello, we’re Will & Owen Cooper, comedy writing brothers who write jokes, sometimes even for money.

We’ve written for radio and TV and we even wrote a book called Great Expectations… wait no, that was Dickens wasn’t it? Sick of Dickens and his bloody books that we didn’t write.

If you’ve got a writing job that needs doing, get in touch to hear about our reasonable rates. Bloody austerity, innit?
